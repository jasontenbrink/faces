First Universalist Church of Minneapolis

In the Universalist Spirit of Love and Hope, We Give, Receive, and Grow

Welcome, User!

Our church, its ministers, and its members have nourished the flame of liberal religion in Minneapolis for more than 150 years. We are numbered among the foremost Unitarian-Universalist congregations in the United States. We invite others of free faith and open mind to join us on our journey.

Our Congregants: First Universalist is a welcoming congregation that draws strength from people of all racial, ethnic and national backgrounds, of all sexual orientations, and of a wide range of abilities.

Our Sunday Service: During the regular church year our services are at 9:30am and 11:15am on Sundays; from Memorial Day weekend through Labor Day weekend our services are at 10am.
